Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

What are your visiting hours?

Our general visiting hours are from 10:00 AM to 8:00 PM every day. However, ICU and CCU have restricted visiting hours. Please check with the specific department for exact timings.

Do you have an emergency room?

Yes, we have a fully equipped 24/7 emergency department staffed with specialized trauma doctors and advanced life support systems.

How can I book an appointment?

You can book an appointment by calling our helpline at 9105106999, using the appointment form on this website, or visiting the hospital reception in person.

Which insurances do you accept?

We accept all major health insurance providers. For a complete list or to verify your specific insurance plan, please contact our billing department.
