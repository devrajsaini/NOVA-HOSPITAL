Our Services

Specialized medical care for every need

Comprehensive healthcare across 15+ departments—delivered by expert specialists using the latest medical technology with a patient‑first approach.

Cardiology

Comprehensive cardiac care from diagnosis to intervention, managed by experienced cardiologists and cardiac surgeons.

  • Interventional cardiology and catheterization lab
  • Cardiac ICU with round‑the‑clock monitoring
  • Non‑invasive diagnostics: ECG, Echo, Stress Test
  • Heart failure management and rehabilitation
View Details

Neurology

Advanced neurological care for brain, spine, and nervous system disorders with cutting‑edge diagnostics and treatment options.

  • Stroke management and cerebrovascular care
  • Epilepsy monitoring and seizure management
  • Neuro‑rehabilitation and physiotherapy
  • Advanced neuro‑imaging: MRI and CT angiography
View Details

Orthopedics

Expert orthopedic care covering joint replacements, trauma, sports injuries, and complex spine surgeries.

  • Total hip, knee, and shoulder replacements
  • Sports injury diagnosis and surgical repair
  • Minimally invasive spine surgery
  • Orthopedic trauma and fracture management
View Details

Pediatrics

Dedicated child‑centered care from newborns to adolescents in a warm, child‑friendly environment.

  • Neonatal ICU (NICU) for premature and sick newborns
  • Pediatric intensive care unit (PICU)
  • Developmental pediatrics and child neurology
  • Childhood vaccinations and preventive care
View Details

Radiology

NABL‑accredited imaging and interventional radiology services using the most advanced equipment available.

  • 3T MRI and 128‑slice CT scanning
  • PET‑CT for oncology and metabolic imaging
  • Digital X‑ray and ultrasound services
  • Interventional radiology procedures
View Details

Emergency Care

24/7 emergency and trauma services with fully equipped resuscitation bays and rapid‑response specialist teams.

  • 24/7 emergency and trauma center
  • Dedicated resuscitation and shock management
  • Emergency surgery and critical care support
  • Ambulance services and pre‑hospital care
View Details
